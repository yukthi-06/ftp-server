# Implementation Plan - Black and White Theme

We will update the application theme, custom color palette, and layout files to implement a strict black and white theme with a white background and black text.

## User Review Required

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The base theme will be changed to a Light theme variant (`Theme.MaterialComponents.Light.NoActionBar`), and the primary brand colors will be remapped to solid black and white. Status colors (running/stopped) will be adapted to black and gray.
